Wednesday, 1st July, 1896.

Numb 305

Agricultural Land Rating Bill,—Order for Third Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read the third time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day three months:"—(Mr. Asquith:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now,' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 292, Noes 140.

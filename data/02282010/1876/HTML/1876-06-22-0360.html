B. M.

Thursday, 22nd June, 1876.

Numb. 152.

Jurors Qualification (Ireland) Bill,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Schedule 1:—Amendment proposed, in page 5, line 6, to leave out the word "fifty," in order to insert words "forty-five:"—(Sir Michael Hicks Beach:)—
Question, "That the word 'fifty' stand part of the Schedule," put, and negatived.
Question proposed, "That the words 'forty-five' be there inserted:"—
Amendment proposed to the said proposed Amendment, to leave out the word "five:"—(Mr. Downing:)—
Question put, "That the word 'five' stand part of the said proposed Amendment:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 195, Noes 105.

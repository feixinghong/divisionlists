Royal Title Bill,—considered in Committee :—
(In the Committee.)
Clause 1:—Amendment proposed, in page 2, line 6, after the word "the," to insert the word "Royal :"—(Mr. Serjeant Simon:)—
Question put, "That the word 'Royal' be there inserted:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 92, Noes 171.

Friday, 30th June, 1876.

Numb. 163.

Supply,—Order for Committee read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, " That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "a Select Committe be appointed to inquire into and report upon the nature, the extent, and the grounds of the demand made by a large proportion of the Irish people for the restoration to Ireland of an Irish Parliament, with power to control the internal affairs of ???t Country :"—(Mr. Bull,)—instead thereof :—
Question put, "That the words poposed to be left out stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 291, Noes 61.

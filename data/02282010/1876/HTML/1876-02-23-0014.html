Wednesday, 23rd February, 1876.

Numb. 7.

Grand Jury Presentments, &c. (Ireland) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made; and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Mr. Kavanagh :)—
Question put, :"That the word 'now' stand part of the Question :"—The divided ; Ayes 153, Noes 181.

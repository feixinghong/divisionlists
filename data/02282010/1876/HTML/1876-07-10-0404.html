Numb. 167.

Elementary Education Bill,—Order for Committee read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, " That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word " That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words " in the opinion of this House, the principle of universal compulsion in Education cannot be applied without great injustice, unless provision be mad for placing public Elementary Schools under public managements,"—(Mr. Richard)—instead thereof :—
Question put, " That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided ; Ayes 317, Noes 99.

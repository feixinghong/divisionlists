Wednesday, 8th March, 1876.

Numb. 42.

Game Laws (Scotland) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Lord Elcho:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 172, Noes 150.

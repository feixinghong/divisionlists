B. M.

Tuesday, 28th March, 1876.

Numb. 64.

Borough Franchise (Ireland),—Motion made, and Question put, "That the restricted nature of the Borough Franchise of Ireland as compared with that existing in England and Scotland is a subject deserving the best attention of Parliament, with a view of establishing a fair and just equality of the Franchise in the three Countries:"—(Mr. Meldon:)—the House divided; Ayes 166, Noes 179.

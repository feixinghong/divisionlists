Thursday, 16th March, 1876.

Numb. 51.

Royal Titles Bill,—Order for Committee read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "while willing to consider a measure enabling Her Majesty to make an addition to the Royal Style and Title, which shall include such Dominions of Her Majesty as to Her Majesty may seem meet, this House is of opinion that it is inexpedient to impair the ancient and Royal Dignity of the Crown by the assumption of the style and title of Emperor,"—(Marquis of Hartington)—instead thereof:—
Question proposed, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—Debate arising;
Motion made, and Question put, "That the Debate be now adjourned:"—(Mr. Thomas Cave:)—The House divided; Ayes 192, Noes 324.

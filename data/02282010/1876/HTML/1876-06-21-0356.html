Wednesday, 21st June, 1876.

Numb. 151.

Banns of Marriage (Scotland) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That, the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day three months:"—(Mr. Orr Ewing :)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided;Ayes 141, Noes 166.

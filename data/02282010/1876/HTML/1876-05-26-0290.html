Friday, 26th May, 1876.

Numb. 120.

Supply,—Order for Committee read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, " That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word " That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "in the opinion of this House, it is undesirable that the benefits intended by Parliament to accrue to any class of Her Majesty's subjects from the passing of any statute should be neutralised by the official action of a Member of the Administration, responsible for the enactment that statute,"—(Dr. Cameron,)—instead thereof:—
Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:—The House divided; Ayes 226, Noes 53.

Wednesday, 26th April, 1876.

Numb. 86

Women's Disabilities Removal Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question propose, "That the Bill be now read second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Viscount Folkestone:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"The House divided; Ayes 152, Noes 239.

B. M.

Tuesday, 15th February, 1876.

Numb. 1.

Municipal Officers Superannuation Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Mr. Fielden:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 101, Noes 94.

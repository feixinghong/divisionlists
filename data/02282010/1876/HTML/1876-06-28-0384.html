Wednesday, 28th June, 1876.

Numb. 161.

Real Estate Intestacy Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day three months:"—(Mr. Gregory:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 175, Noes 210.

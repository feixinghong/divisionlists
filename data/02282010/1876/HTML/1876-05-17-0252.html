Wednesday, 17th May, 1876.

Numb. 106

Intoxicating Liquors (Licensing Boards)Bill;—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now" and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months;"—(Sir Walter Burttelot:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question;"—The House divided; Ayes 109, Noes 274.

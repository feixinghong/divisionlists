Wenesday, 16th February, 1876

Numb. 2.

Increase of the Episcopate Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Whereupon Previous Question proposed. " That the Question be now put:"—(Sir Walter Barttelot:)—Debate arising;
Motion made and Question put, " That the Debate be now adjourned:"—(Mr. Hull:)— The House divided; Ayes 174, Noes 90.

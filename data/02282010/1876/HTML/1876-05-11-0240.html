Thursday, 11th May, 1876.

Numb. 103.

Royal Titles Act Proclamation,—Motion made, and Question put "That, having regard to the declarations made by Her Majesty's Ministers during the progress of the Royal Titles Act through Parliament, this House is of opinion that the Proclamation issued by virtue of that Act does not make adequate provision for restraining and preventing the use of the title of Empress in relation to the internal affairs of Her Majesty's dominions other than India:"—(Sir Henry James:)—The House divided; Ayes 226, Noes 334.

Mortis, 3e die Martii, 1846.
Customs, and Corn Importation,-(In the Committee) .-Motion made, and Question proposed, "That, in
lieu of the Duties now payable on the importation of Corn, Grain, Meal, or Flour, there shall be
paid, until the 1st day of February 1849, the following Duties :"-Amendment proposed, to leave
out the words " in lieu of," in order, to insert the words " all Duties on imported Corn do now
cease and determine,", instead thereof:-(Mr. Villiers:)-Question put, "That the words pro
posed to be left out stand part of the Question :"-The Committee divided; Ayes 265, Noes 78.

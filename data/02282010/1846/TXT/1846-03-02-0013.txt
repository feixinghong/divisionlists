Customs, and Corn Importation,-(In the Committee) :-Morion made, and Question proposed, "That, in
lieu of the Duty now payable on the importation of Corn, Grain, Meal, or Flour, there shall be
paid1, until the 1st day of February 1849, the following Duties -."-Amendment proposed, to leave
out the words " in lieu of," in order to insert the words " all Duties on imported Corn do now
cease and determine," instead thereof:-(Mr. Villiers:)-Question proposed, "That the words
proposed to be left out stand part of the Question ;"-Whereupon, Motion made and Question
put, " That the Chairman do report progress, and ask leave to sit again :"-The Committee divided;
Ayes 70, Noes 227·

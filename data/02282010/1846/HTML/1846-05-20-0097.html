Mercurii, 20° die Maii, 1846.

Numb. 49.

Lace Factories Bill,—Order for Second Reading read ; Motion made, and Question proposed,—" That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day six months :"—Question put, " That the word 'now' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 66, Noes 151.

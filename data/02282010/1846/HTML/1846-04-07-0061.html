Martis, 7° die Aprilis, 1846.

Numb. 32.

General Terminus and Glasgow Harbour Railway Bill,—Report considered; Amendments read 2°: Motion made, and Question proposed, "That this House doth agree with the Committee in the said Amendments:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "the Minutes of the Evidence taken before the Committee on the Bill be laid before this House ;"—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 74, Noes 82.

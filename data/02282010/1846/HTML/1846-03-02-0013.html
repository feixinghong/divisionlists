Lunæ, 2° die Martii, 1846.

Numb. 5.

Customs, and Corn Importation,—(In the Committee):—Motion made, and Question proposed, " That, in lieu of the Duty now payable on the importation of Corn, Grain, Meal, or Flour, there shall be paid, until the 1st day of February 1849, the following Duties:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the words " in lieu of," in order to insert the words " all Duties on imported Corn do now cease and determine," instead thereof:—(Mr. Villiers:)—Question proposed, " That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question;"—Whereupon, Motion made and Question put, " That the Chairman do report progress, and ask leave to sit again:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 70, Noes 227.

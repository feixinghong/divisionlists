Jovis, 25° die Junii, 1846.

Protection of Life (Ireland) Bill,—Order read, for resuming adjourned Debate on Amendment proposed to be made to Question [8th June], "That the Bill be now read a second time," and which Amendment was to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question again proposed, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question :" —Debate resumed:—Question put:—The House divided; Ayes 219, Noes 292.

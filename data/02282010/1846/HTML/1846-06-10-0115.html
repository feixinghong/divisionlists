Mercurii, 10° die Junii, 1846.

Numb. 58.

Danish Claims,—Order for Committee read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "this House will, upon this day six months, resolve itself into the said Committee," instead thereof:—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 58, Noes 85.

Jovis, 11° die Junii, 1846.

Numb. 59.

South Yorkshire Coal Railway and Canal Bill,—Report considered; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be re-committed:"—Debate arising; Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That the Debate be now adjourned:"—Motion, by leave, withdrawn:—Main Question put:—The House divided; Ayes 41, Noes 51.

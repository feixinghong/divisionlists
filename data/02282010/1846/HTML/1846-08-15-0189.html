Sabbati, 15° die Augusti, 1846.

Numb. 101.

British Possessions Bill,—Order for Third Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read the third time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question, to add the words "upon this day three months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 47, Noes 8.

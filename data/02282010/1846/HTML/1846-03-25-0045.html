Second Clause (Not to be lawful for Justices of the Peace to grant to the tacksman of tolls or toll gatherer, or other person situated at any toll bar, any license to sell any wine, ale, spirituous liquors, or provisions):—
Amendment proposed, at the end of the Clause to add the words, "at any toll bar within such shire, stewartry, or royal burgh respectively :"

Lunæ, 27° die Julii, 1846.

Numb. 84.

Lancaster and Preston Junction Railway Bill,—Report considered; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Amendments made by the Committee to the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the words "That the" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "Report be taken into further Consideration upon this day six months," instead thereof:—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 6, Noes 71.

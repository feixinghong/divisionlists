Numb. 7.

Mercurii, 4° die Martii, 1846.

Bequests for Pious and Charitable Purposes Bill,—Order for Second Reading read ; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months :" —Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 24 ; Noes 60.

Mercurii, 11° die Martii, 1846.

Numb. 10.

Roman Catholic Relief Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided ; Ayes 66, Noes 23.

Jovis, 6° die Augusti, 1846.

Numb. 93.

Religious Opinions Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day three months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 79, Noes 10.

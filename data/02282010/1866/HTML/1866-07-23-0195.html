Monday, 23rd July, 1866.

Numb. 68.

Supply,—Order for Committee read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question in order to add the words "it is desirable that a new National Gallery be erected on the site of the Burlington House Gardens,"—(Mr. Beresford Hope,)—instead thereof:—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 94, Noes 17.

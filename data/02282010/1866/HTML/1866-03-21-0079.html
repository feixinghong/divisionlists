Wednesday, 21st March, 1866.

Numb. 30.

Tests Abolition (Oxford) Bill, — Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time;"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Sir Stafford Northcote:)—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 217, Noes 103.

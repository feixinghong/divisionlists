Thursday, 3rd May, 1866.

Numb. 39.

Grand Juries Presentment (Ireland) (re-committed) Bill,—considered in Committee :—
(In the Committee)
New Clause (In absence of justice, &c.,)—(Lord John Browne,)—brought up, and read the first and second time:—Amendment proposed, in line 1, after the word "any," to insert the words "presentment or:"—(Colonel Vandeleur:)—Question put, "That those words be there inserted:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 5, Noes 54.

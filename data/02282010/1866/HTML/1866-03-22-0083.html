Thursday, 22nd March, 1866.

???umb. 31.

Supply,—Order for Committee read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That Mr. Deputy Speaker do now leave the Chair:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "in the opinion of this House, it is not expedient that the competition for the building of the New Courts of Justice should be limited to six architects only,"—(Mr. Bentinck,)—instead thereof:—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 70, Noes 101.

Thursday, 19th July, 1866.

Numb. 67.

Thames Navigation (re-committed) Bill,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
New Clause (Treasury to determine compensation,)—(Mr. Childers,)—brought up, and read the first time:—Motion made, and Question put, "That the said Clause be now read a second time:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 62, Noes 24.

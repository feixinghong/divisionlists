Wednesday, 2nd May, 1866.

Numb. 38.

Marriage with a Deceased Wife's Sister Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six month:"—(Mr. Hunt:)—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 155, Noes 174.

Thursday, 26th April, 1866.

Numb. 36.

Contagious Diseases (re-committed) Bill,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Clause 15:—Amendment proposed, in line 18, after the word "prostitute," to insert the words "and affected with a contagious disease:"—(Mr. Ayrton:) —Question put, "That those words be there inserted:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 5, Noes 76.

Monday, 28th May, 1866.

Numb. 46.

Representation of the People Bill,—Order for Committee read:—
Ordered, That the Representation of the People Bill and the Redistribution of Seats Bill be referred to the same Committee:—Instruction to the Committee, that they have power to consolidate the said Bills into one Bill.—(Mt. Bouverie.)
Motion made, and Question put, "That it be an Instruction to the Committee that they have power to make provision for the better prevention of bribery and corruption at Elections:"—(Sir Rainald Knightley:)—The House divided; Ayes 248, Noes 238.

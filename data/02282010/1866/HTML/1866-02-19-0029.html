Monday, 19th February, 1866.

Numb. 7.

Cattle Plague (re-committed) Bill,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Clause 14:—Amendment proposed, at the end of the Clause, to add the words "and with a licence for any longer distance and until such license shall be revoked by any justice of the peace:"—(Mr. Bovill:)—Question proposed, "That those words be there added:"—Amendment proposed to the said proposed Amendment to leave out the words "by any justice of the peace:"—Question, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the said proposed Amendment," put, and negatived:—Question put, "That the words ' and with a licence for any longer distance and until such licence shall be revoked' be added at the end of Clause 14:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 146, Noes 83.

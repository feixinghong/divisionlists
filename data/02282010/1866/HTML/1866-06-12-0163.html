Tuesday, 12th June, 1866.

Numb. 58.

Gaslight and Coke Company Bill (by Order),—Order for Consideration, as amended, read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now taken into Consideration:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day three months:"—(Mr. Tite:)—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 169, Noes 138.

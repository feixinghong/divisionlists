Numb. 6.

Cattle Diseases Bill,—As amended, considered:—
Clause (The powers of each Cattle Board shall extend over the whole of the county for which it is appointed, and over all burghs situated therein, excepting cities and burghs which have the right to elect Members to Parliament, and whereof the population within the parliamentary boundaries of any such burgh exceeds ten thousand,)—(Sir James Fergusson,)—brought up, and read the first time :—Motion made, and Question put, "That the said Clause be now read a second time:"—The House divided; Ayes 100, Noes 146.

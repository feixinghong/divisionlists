A. M.

Friday, 21st May, 1886.

Numb. 103.

Cottagers' Allotment Gardens Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Mr. Arch:)—
Question proposed, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question"—Debate arising;
Motion made, and Question put, "That the Debate be now adjourned:"—(Mr. Johns:)—The House divided; Ayes 106, Noes 91.

Monday, 31st May, 1886.

Numb. 112.

Medical Acts Amendment Bill,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Amendment proposed, in page 4, line 27, to leave out from the word "London," to the word "collectively," in line 29, in order to insert the words—
"The Victoria University;
The University of Durham:"—(Sir Henry Roscoe:)—
Question, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Clause,"put, and negatived.
Question put, "That those words be there inserted:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 119, Noes 42.

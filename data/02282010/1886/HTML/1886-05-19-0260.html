Wednesday, 19th May, 1886.

Numb. 99.

Poor Law Guardians (Ireland) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Colonel King-Harman:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 203, Noes 105.

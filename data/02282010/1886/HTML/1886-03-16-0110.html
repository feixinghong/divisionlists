B. M.

Tuesday, 16th March, 1886.

Numb. 35.

Contagious Diseases Acts, 1866-1869,—Motion made, and Question proposed, "That, in the opinion of this House, the Contagious Diseases Acts, 1866-1869, ought to be repeated:"—(Mr. Stansfeld:)—
Amendment proposed, at the end of the Question, to add the words "due provision at the same time being made for the continued maintenance of hospital accommodation with adequate treatment of women voluntarily seeking admission and medical care:"— (Sir John Kennaway:)—
Question put, "That those words be there added:"—The House divided; Ayes 131, Noes 245.

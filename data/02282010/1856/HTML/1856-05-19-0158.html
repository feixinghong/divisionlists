Numb. 73.

Fire Insurances Bill,—Order for Committee read ; Motion made, and Question proposed, " That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words " this House will, upon this day six months, resolve itself into the said Committee," instead thereof :—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 172, Noes 31.

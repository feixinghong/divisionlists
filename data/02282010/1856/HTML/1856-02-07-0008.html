Jovis, 7° die Februarii, 1856.

Numb. 2.

Naval Administration,—Motion made, and Question put, "That a Select Committee be appointed to inquire into the Naval Administration and the lists of officers, patronage, promotions and retirements, and the efficiency of the Service in all the grades belonging to it:"—(Captain Scobell:)—The House divided; Ayes 80, Noes 171.

Martis, 26° die Februarii, 1856.

Numb. 11.

Court of Chancery (Ireland) Bill,—Select Committee nominated:—Mr. Solicitor General for Ireland, Sir James Graham, Mr. Edward Ellice, Mr. Henley, Mr. Walpole, Mr. Keogh, Viscount Monck:— Motion made, and Question, "That Mr. Cairns be one other Member of the said Committee," put, and negatived :—Mr. Napier, Mr. Macartney, Mr. Henry Herbert, Mr. William Fitzgerald, Mr. Whiteside, to be Members of the said Committee:— Motion made, and Question proposed, " That Mr. De Vere be one other Member of the said Committee :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the name of " Mr. De Vere " and to insert the name of " Sir FitzRoy Kelly," instead thereof :— Question," That the name of Mr. De Vere stand part of the Question," put, and agreed to :—Mr. De Vere to be one other Member of the said Committte :—Motion made, and Question put," That Sir Erskine Perry be one other Member of the said Committee :"—The House divided ; Ayes 128, Noes 69.

Lunæ, 10° die Martii, 1856.

Numb. 17.

Police (Counties and Boroughs) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read ; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Mr. Charles Forster:)—Question put,"That the word 'now' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 259, Noes 106.

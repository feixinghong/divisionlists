Martis, 27° die Maii, 1856.

Numb. 88.

Savings Banks (Tralee and Killarney),—Motion made, and Question proposed, "That this House will, upon Friday next; resolve itself into a Committee to consider an Address to Her Majesty, praying that She will be graciously pleased to extend the same measure of relief to the depositors in the late Tralee and Killarney Savings Banks as has been already extended to the depositors in the late Cuffe Street Savings Bank :" — (Captain Daniel O'Connell :)— Amendment proposed, to leave out the words " the same," in order to insert the words "a full,"— (Mr. Vance,)—instead thereof: —Question proposed, "That the words the same, stand part of the Question :"— Debate arising; Motion made, and Question, "That the Debate be now adjourned," —(Mr. Hankey) — put, and negatived :— Question put, " That the words the same stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 84, Noes 9.

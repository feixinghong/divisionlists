Mercurii, 25° die Junii, 1856.

Numb. 144.

Maynooth College Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day three months:"—Question put, "That the word ' now,' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided ; Ayes 174, Noes 168.

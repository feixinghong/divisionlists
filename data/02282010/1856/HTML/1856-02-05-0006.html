Martis, 5° die Februarii, 1856.

Numb. 1.

Business of the House,—Motion made, and Question put, "That, on those days on which the House shall sit in the Evening, the Debate on any business under discussion at Twelve o'clock (midnight) shall stand adjourned to the next day on which the House shall sit ; after which no opposed business shall be proceeded with :
And that, whenever the House shall be in Committee on any Evening at midnight, the Committee do report progress, and Mr. Speaker do resume the Chair :"—(Mr. Brotherton:)—The House divided ; Ayes 50, Noes 111.

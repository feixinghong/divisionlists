Jovis, 10° die Julii, 1856.

Numb. 171.

Appellate Jurisdiction (House of Lords) Bill [Lords],—Order for Committee read ; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair:" —Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "the Bill be committed to a Select Committee,"—(Mr. Raikes Currie,)—instead thereof:—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 133, Noes 155.

Martis, 15° die Julii, 1856.

Numb. 176.

Militia Pay Bill,—considered in Committee :—
(In the Committee.)
Clause Three (Adjutants, Quartermasters, and Non-Commissioned Officers of Militia may be employed in their counties) :—Question put, " That the Clause stand part of the Bill :"—The Committee divided ; Ayes 62, Noes 44.

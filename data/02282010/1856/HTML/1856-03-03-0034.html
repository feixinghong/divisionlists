Lunæ, 3° die Martii, 1856.

Numb. 14.

Imperial Hotel Company Bill,—Order for Second Reading read ; Motion made, and Question proposed," That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day six months :"—Question put," That the word ' now ' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 72. Noes 64.

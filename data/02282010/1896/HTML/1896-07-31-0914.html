Numb. 360.

Dublin Corporation Bill [Lords] (by Order),—Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now considered:"—(Dr. Farquharson :)—Debate arising;
Whereupon Amendment proposed, to leave out the words "now considered,"in order to add the words "re-committed to the former Committee:"—(Mr. Harrington :)—
Question, "That the words 'now considered' stand part of the Question," put, and negatived.
Question proposed, "That the words "re-committed to the former Committee' be there added:"—
Amendment proposed to the proposed Amendment, to leave out the words "to the former Committee:"—(Colonel Gunter :)—
Question, "That the words 'to the former Committee' stand part of the proposed Amendment," put, and negatived.
Another Amendment proposed to the proposed Amendment, after the word "re-committed," to add the words "in respect of Clauses 15 and 23 only to re-consider the same and bring them within the scope of the Instructions of the House:"—(Mr. T. M. Healy :)—
Question put, "That those words be there added:" — The House divided ; Ayes 83, Noes 139.

B. M.

Friday, 7th August, 1896.

Numb. 393.

Supply,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
1. Motion made, and Question proposed, "That a sum, not exceeding £100, be granted to Her Majesty, to defray the Charge for the Ordinance factories (the cost of the Productions of which will be charged to the Army, Navy, and Indian and Colonial Governments), which will come in coarse of payment during the year ending on the 31st day of March 1897 :"—
Whereupon Motion made, and Question put, "That a sum, not exceeding £50, be granted for the said Service:"—(Captain Norton :)—The Committee divided ; Ayes 57, Noes 145.

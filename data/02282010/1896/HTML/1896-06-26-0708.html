Friday, 26th June, 1896.

Numb. 280

Supply,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Navy Estimates, 1896-7.
4. Motion made, and Question proposed, "That a sum, not exceeding £236,800, be granted to Her Majesty, to defray the Expenses of the Admiralty Office, which will come in course of payment during the year ending on the 31st day of March 1897:"—
Whereupon Motion made, and Question put, "That Item A (Salaries), be reduced by £1,500, in respect of the Salary of the First Lord of the Admiralty:"—(Mr. W???ir:)—
The Committee proceeded to a Division:—
The Chairman stated that he thought the Noes had it; and on his decision being challenged, it appeared to him that the Division was frivolously claimed; and he accordingly directed the Ayes to stand up in their places, and ten Members having stood up, the Chairman declared that the Noes had it.

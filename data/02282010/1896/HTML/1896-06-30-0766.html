West Highland Railway Guarantee Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That," to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "it is undesirable to assist in making any railway by a special guarantee which would differ materially from the assistance to be given under the Light Railways Bill now before this House,"—(Mr. Strachey,)— instead thereof:—
Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 235, Noes 67.

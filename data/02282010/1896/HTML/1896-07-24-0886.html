Supply,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Civil Services and Revenue Departments Estimates, 1896-7.
Class IV.
1. Motion made, and Question proposed, "That a sum, not exceeding £616,077 (including a Supplementary sum of £3,375), be granted to Her Majesty, to compete the sum necessary to defray the Charge which will come in course of payment during the year ending on the 31st day of March 1897, for the Expenses of the Commissioners of National Education in Ireland:"—
Whereupon Motion made, and Question put, "That Item 1 (School Grants) be reduced by £50:"—(Mr. Knox :)—The Committee divided; Ayes 105, Noes 154.

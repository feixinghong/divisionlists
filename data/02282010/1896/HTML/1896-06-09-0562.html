B. M.

Tuesday, 9th June, 1896.

Numb. 227.

Supply,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Civil Services and Revenue Departments Estimates, 1896-7.
Class III.
1. Motion made, and Question proposed, "That a sum, not exceeding £781,992, be granted to Her Majesty, to complete the sum necessary to defray the Charge which will come in course of payment during the year ending on the 31st day of March 1897, for the Expenses of the Royal Irish Constabulary:"—
Whereupon Motion made, and Question put, "That a sum, not exceeding £281,992, be granted for the said Service:"—(Mr. William Redmond:)—The Committee divided; Ayes 70, Noes 156.

Jovis, 12° die Maii, 1836.

Joint Stock Banks Committee,—Motion made, and Question put,—"That it be an Instruction to the Committee, that they do inquire into the operation of the privilege conferred on Country and Joint Stock Banks to pay their Promissory Notes in Bank of England Notes, instead of paying them in gold."—(Mr. Hume :)—The House divided; Ayes 12, Noes 98.

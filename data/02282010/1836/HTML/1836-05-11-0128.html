Mercurii, 11° die Maii, 1836.

Roman Catholic Marriages Bill,—Order for Third Reading read;—Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That the Bill be now read the third time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 100, Noes 91.

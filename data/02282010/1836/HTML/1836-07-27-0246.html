Mercurii, 27° die Julii, 1836.

Hackney Carriages (Metropolis) Bill,—Order for Committee read ; Motion made, and Question proposed,—" That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word " That " to the end of the Question, in order to add the words " this House do resolve itself into the said Committee upon this day six months :"—Question put, " That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 45, Noes 5.

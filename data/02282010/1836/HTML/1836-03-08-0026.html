Martis, 8° die Martii, 1836.

Municipal Corporations (Ireland) Bill,—Order for resuming adjourned Debate on Question proposed [7th March], "That it be an instruction to the Committee, that they have power to make provision for the abolition of such Corporations, and for such arrangements as may be necessary, on their abolition, for securing the efficient and impartial administration of justice, and the peace and good government of Cities and Towns in Ireland:"—Question again stated:—Question put:—The House divided;Ayes 243, Noes 307.

Mercurii, 4° die Maii, 1836.

Poor Rate Bill,—Order for Second Reading read ; Motion made, and Question propesed,—" That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words" upon this day six months:"—Question put,"That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided ; Ayes, 41, Noes 13.

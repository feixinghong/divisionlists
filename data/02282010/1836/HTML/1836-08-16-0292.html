Martis, 16° die Augusti, 1836.

Common Fields Inclosure Bill,—Lords Amendments to be considered To-morrow:—Motion made, and Question proposed.—"That the said Amendments be printed:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word " the" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words, "Bill as amended by the Lords be printed :"—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 24, Noes 3.

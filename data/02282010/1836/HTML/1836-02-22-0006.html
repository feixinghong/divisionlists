Lunæ, 22° die Februarii, 1836.

London and Brighton Railway (Stevenson's Line),—Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendments proposd, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon Monday next:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided:—

Jovis, 10° die Martii, 1836.

Spirit Licenses,—Act [4 and 5 Will. 4, c. 75] read; Motion made, and Question proposed;—"That this House do resolve itself into a Committee of the whole House, with a view to take into its consideration the propriety of an immediate repeal of such portion of the Act 4 and 5 Will. 4, c. 75, as imposes an additional Duty of 50 per cent. on retail Spirit Licenses:"—(Mr. Divett:)— Whereupon, Motion made, and Question put, "That the Debate on this question be adjourned until Thursday the 15th day of April next:"—The House divided; Ayes 165, Noes 155:

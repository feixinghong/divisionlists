Mercurii, 13° die Julii, 1836.

Hackney Carriages Metropolis Bill,—Order for Committee read ; Motion made, and Question proposed, —"That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words " this House do resolve itself into the said Committee upon this day six months :"—Question " That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 118, Noes 2.

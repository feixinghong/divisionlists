Monday, 7th May, 1906.

Numb. 69.

Housing of the Working Classes Acts Amendment Bill,—Motion made, and Question proposed, "That Sir John Dickson-Poynder be a Member of the Select Committee on the Housing of the Working Classes Acts Amendment Bill:"—(Mr. Whiteley:)—
And, the Motion being opposed, after a brief statement from a Member who opposed the Motion, and a reply from the Member who made it, Mr. Speaker put the Question thereon pursuant to Standing Order No. 11:—The House divided; Ayes 335, Noes 85.

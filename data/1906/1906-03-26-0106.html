Numb. 27.

Evening Sitting.
Wireless Telegraphy Bill,—As amended, considered:—
Amendment proposed to the Bill, in page 1, line 6, to leave out the word "twelve," and insert the word "eight:"—(Mr. Claude Hay:)—
Question put, "That the word 'twelve' stand part of the Bill:"—The House divided: Ayes 293, Noes 39.

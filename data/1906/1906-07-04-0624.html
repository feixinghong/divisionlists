Friday, 6th July, 1906.

Numb. 192.

Town Tenants (Ireland) Bill,—Order read, for resuming Adjourned Debate on Question [18th May], " That the Bill be committed to the Standing Committee on Law, &c.:"—(Mr. M'Killop :)—
Question again proposed :—Debate resumed :—
Question amended, by leaving out the word "Law," and inserting the word "Trade,"—(Mr. John Redmond,)—instead thereof :—
Main Question, as amended, proposed :—Debate arising ;
Mr. James Bryce rose in his place, and claimed to move, "That the Question be now put :"—
Question put, "That the Question be now put :"—The House divided; Ayes 255, Noes 66.

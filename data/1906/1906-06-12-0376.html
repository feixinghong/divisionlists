(In the Committee.)
Clause 2:—
Another Amendment proposed, in page 1, line 18, after the word "manner," to insert the words "where any existing voluntary school is not taken over by the local education authority, owing to the authority having made no proposal or come to no arrangement under this section, the owners or trustees of such school may appeal to the Board of Education, and the Board shall then hold a local inquiry, and if, in the opinion of the Board, the local education authority has without good and sufficient reason failed to make any proposal or arrangement for carrying on such school as a provided school, the Board may require the local education authority to take over such school on such terms as the Board may determine:"—(Major Seely:)—

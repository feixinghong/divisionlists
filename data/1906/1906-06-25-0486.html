Monday 25th June, 1906

Numb. 150.

Great Northern Railway Bill (by Order);—Order read, for resuming Adjourned Debate on Question [19th June], "That the Bill, as amended, be now considered:"—
Question again proposed:—Debate resumed ;—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now" and at the end of the Question to and the words "upon this day three months:"—(Mr. Mort???)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 165, Noes 114.

Friday, 30th November, 1906.

Numb. 455.

Town Tenants (Ireland) Bill,—Order for Third Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read the third time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day three months:"—(Viscount Castlereagh:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 201, Noes 28.

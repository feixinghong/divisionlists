Education (England and Wales) Bill,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Clause 1 :—
Another Amendment proposed, in page 1, line 10, at the end, to add the words,—
"And unless provision is made that religious instruction shall not be given therein during school hours, nor at the public expense:"—(Mr. Maddison :)—
Amendment proposed to the proposed Amendment, to leave out the words "during school hours, nor:"—(Mr. Chamberlain :)—
Question again proposed, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the proposed Amendment:"—Debate arising;
Mr. Birrell rose in his place, and claimed to move, "That the Question be now put:"—
Question put, "That the Question be now put:"—The Committee divided; Aves 366, Noes 179.

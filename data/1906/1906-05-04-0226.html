Friday, 4th May, 1906.

Numb. 68.

Light Dues (Abolition) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read ;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time :"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months :"(Mr. Harold Cox :)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 142, Noes 169.

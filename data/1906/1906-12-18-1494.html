Tuesday, 18th December, 1906.

Numb. 500.

Public Trustee Bill [Lords],—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Clause 4:—
Another Amended proposed, in page 3, line 25, after the word "trustee," to insert the words "if expressly authorised in that behalf by the will, settlement, or other instrument creating the trust:"—(Mr. Micklem:)—
Question put, "That those words be there inserted:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 17, Noes 213.

B. M.

Thursday, 29th November, 1906.

Numb. 454.

Workmen's Compensation Bill,—As amended, further considered :—
Another Amendment proposed to the Bill, in page 2, line 8, after the word "shall," to insert the words "unless the injury results in death or serious and permanent disablement :"—(Mr. Secretary Gladstone :)—
Question put, "That those words be there inserted in the Bill:"—The House divided; Ayes 286, Noes 27.

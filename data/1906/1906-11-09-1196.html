Friday, 9th November, 1906.

Numb. 391.

Trade Disputes Bill,—Order for Third Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read the third time :"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out all the words after the word "be," and add the words "re-committed in respect of Clause 5:"—(Lord Robert Cecil :)—
Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 220, Noes 46.

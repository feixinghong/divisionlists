Monday, 19th March, 1906.

Numb. 18.

Evening Sitting.
Supply [3rd allotted Day],—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Army Estimates, 1906-7.
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That a sum, not exceeding £10,220,000, be granted to His Majesty, to defray the Charge for the Pay, &c., of His Majesty's Army (including Army Reserve) at Home and Abroad (exclusive of India), which will come in course of payment during the year ending on the 31st day of March 1907:"—Debate arising;
Mr. John Ward rose in his place, and claimed to move, "That the Question be now put:"—
Question put, "That the Question be now put:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 222, Noes 36.

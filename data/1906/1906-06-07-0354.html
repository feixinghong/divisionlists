Supply [10th allotted Day],—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Army Estimates, 1906-7.
5. Whereupon Motion made, and Question put, "That a sum, not exceeding £2,352,900, be granted for the said Service:"—(Sir William Evans-Gordon)—The Committee divided; Ayes 19, Noes 134.

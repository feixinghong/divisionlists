Wednesday, 25th July, 1866.

Numb. 71.

Public Libraries Act Amendment (re-committed) Bill,—considered in Committee :—
(In the Committee.)
Clause (Libraries and Museums exempted from rates,)—(Mr. Cheetham,)—brought up, and read, the first time :—Motion made, and Question put, " That the Clause be read a second time :"— The Committee divided ; Ayes 10, Noes 54.

Wednesday, 28th February, 1866.

Numb. 13.

Qualification for Offices Abolition Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time: "—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Mr. Newdegate:)—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 176, Noes 55.

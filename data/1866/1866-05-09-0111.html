Wednesday, 9th May, 1866.

Numb. 40.

Public Companies Bill,—Order read, for resuming Adjourned Debate on Amendment proposed to the Bill [7th May] (on Consideration, as amended), and which Amendment was, in page 1, line 18, to leave out the words " o??? at the poll:"—(Sir James Fergusson:)—Question again proposed, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Bill:"—Debate resumed:—Question put:—The House divided; Ayes 46, Noes 53.

Monday, 30th July, 1866.

Numb. 77.

Reformatory Schools (re-committed) Bill,—considered in Committee :—
(In the Committee.)
Clause 14 :—Amendment proposed, in page 5, line 8, after the word "guardian," to insert the words "or if there be no parent, step-parent, or guardian, then the god-parent:"—(Mr. Attorney General :)—Question put, "That those words be there inserted:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 83, Noes 49.

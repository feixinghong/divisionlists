Wednesday, 11th April, 1866.

Numb. 33.

Valuation of Lands and Heritages (Scotland) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Mr. Adam:)—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:—The House divided; Ayes 33, Noes 80.

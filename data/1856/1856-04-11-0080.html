Veneris, 11° die Aprilis, 1856.

Numb. 38.

Education,—Matter considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Question again proposed, "That, in the opinion of this Committee, it is expedient to extend, revise, and consolidate tha Minutes of the Committee of Privy Council on Education:"—(Lord John Russell:)—Whereupon Question again proposed, "That the Chairman do now leave the Chair:"— (Mr. Henley:)—Motion made, and Question, " That the Chairman do report progress, and ask leave to sit again," —(Mr. Baines,)—put, and negatived:—Question put, " That the Chairman do now leave the Chair:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 260, Noes 158.

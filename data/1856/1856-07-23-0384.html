Mercurii, 23° die Julii, 1856.

Numb. 194.

Bishops of London and Durham Retirement Bill [Lords],—Order read, for resuming Adjourned Debate on Question [22d July], "That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Question again proposed :— Debate resumed:—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now." and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day three months :"—(Sir William Heathcote :)—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 151, Noes 72.

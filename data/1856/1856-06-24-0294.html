Martis, 24° die Junii, 1856.

Numb. 143.

Nawab of Surat Treaty Bill,—Order for Third Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read the third time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day three months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 213, Noes 28.

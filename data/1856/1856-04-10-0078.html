Jovis, 10° die Aprilis, 1856.

Numb. 37.

Local Charges upon Shipping Committee,—Ordered, That the Committee do consist of Seventeen Members: —Mr. Vance added to the Committee.
Motion made, and Question proposed, " That, Mr. Kirk be added to the Committee :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the name of Mr. Kirk, in order to insert the name of Mr. Beamish,—(Mr. Vance,)—instead thereof :—Question put, "That the name of Mr. Kirk stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 73, Noes 94.

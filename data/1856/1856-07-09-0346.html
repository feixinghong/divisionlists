Judgments Execution, &c. Bill,— Order for Committee read ; Motion made, and Question proposed, " That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word " That " to the end of the Question, in order to add the words " this House will, upon this day three months, resolve itself into the said Committee,"—instead thereof : — Question put, " That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 74, Noes 69.

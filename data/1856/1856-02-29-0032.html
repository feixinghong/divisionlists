Veneris, 29° die Februarii, 1856.

Numb. 13.

London Diocesan Church Building Society Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day six months:"—Question put,"That the word ' now ' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 135, Noes 20.

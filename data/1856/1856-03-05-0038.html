Mercurii, 5° die Martii, 1856.

Numb. 16.

Church Rates Abolition Bill,—Order for Second Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word, "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 221, Noes 178.

Numb. 6.
Contractors' Disqualification Removal,-Motion made, and Question put, "That leave be given to bring in
a Bill to repeal the Act 22 Geo. 3, disqualifying Contractors from being elected to, or sitting and
voting as Members in, the House of Commons:"-(Mr. Thomas Mitchell:)-The House divided;
Ayes 46, Noes 43.

Lunæ, 21° die Julii, 1856.

Numb. 190.

Joint Stock Banks Bill,—Order for Third Reading read; Motion mode, and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read the third time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day three months:"—Question put, " That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 92, Noes 12.

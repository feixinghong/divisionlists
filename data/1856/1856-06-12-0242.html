Jovis, 12° die Junii, 1856.

Numb. 119.

Peace Preservation (Ireland) Bill [Lords],—Order for Second Reading read ; Motion made, and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day six months :" —Question put, " That the word ' now ' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 77, Noes 10.

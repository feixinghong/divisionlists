Lunœ, 21° die Aprilis, 1856.

Numb. 52.

Supply,—considered in Committee :—
(In the Committee.)
2. Motion made, and Question proposed, "That a sum, not exceeding £18,626, be granted to Her Majesty, to defray the Expense of the General Board of Health, to the 31st day of March 1857:"
Whereupon Motion made, and Question, "That a sum, not exceeding £6,266, be granted to Her Majesty, to defray the Expense of the General Board of Health, to the 31st day of March 1857,"—(Mr. Michell,)—put, and negatived.
Original Question again proposed :—Whereupon Motion made, and Question put, " That a sum, not exceeding £18,326, he granted to Her Majesty, to defray the Expense of the General Board of Health, to the 31st day of March 1857:"—(Mr. Miles:)—The Committee divided; Ayes 63, Noes 154.

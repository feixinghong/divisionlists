Martis, 15° die Martii, 1836.

Soap and Tallow Duties,—Customs and Excise Acts read; Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That this House will resolve itself into a Committee of the whole House on the Customs and Excise Acts, to consider of the expediency of repealing the Duties on Soap, and increasing the Duty of Customs on the importation of Foreign Tallow:"—Question put:—The House divided; Ayes 125, Noes 195.

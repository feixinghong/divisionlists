Lunæ, 28° die Martii, 1836.

Municipal Corporations (Ireland) Bill,—Order for Third Reading read; Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That the Bill be now read the third time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question put,"That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided ; Ayes 260, Noes 199.

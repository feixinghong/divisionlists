Jovis, 28° die Aprilis, 1836

Edinburgh Poor Rates Bill,—reported; Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That the Amendments be now read a second time:"—Amendment proposed to leave out the words "That the" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "Report be taken into consideration upon this day six months:"—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 108, Noes 77:—Amendments read a second time, and agreed to:—Bill to be ingrossed.

Lunæ, 18° die Julii, 1836.

Paper Duties Bill,—read 3° ; Amendment proposed, in Pr. 30, line 10, after the word "Excise" to insert the following Proviso :—"Provided always, that, until the eleventh day of October, one thousand eight hundred and thirty-eight, nothing herein contained shall extend to subject to a Duty, or to any regulation of the Excise, any goods, wares, or articles manufactured under a Patent bearing date the fourteenth day of February, in the third year of the reign of His present Majesty, granted to Thomas Robertson Williams, esquire, late of Norfolk Street, Strand, for securing to the said Thomas Robertson Williams, esquire, late of Norfolk Street, Strand, for securing to the said Thomas Robertson Williams, and his assigns, the benefit of an invention, as set forth in the said Patent, of a new combination of fibrous materials, forming by means of machinery, artificial skins, which may be applied to the purposes for which skins, leather, vellum, and parchment, are used, and which Patent is now by assignment vested in Charles Stanbridge and William Forbes Marshall, of the parish of Saint Luke, in the county of Middlesex :"—Amendment proposed to the said Proviso, to leave out the words " until the eleventh day of October, one thousand eight hundred and thirty-eight, in order to insert the words "during the continuance of their Patent " instead thereof :—Question put," That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the proviso:"—The House divided ; Ayes 56, Noes 22.

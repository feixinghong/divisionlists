Jovis, 24° die Martii, 1836.

Todmorden (Lancaster) Inclosure Bill,—Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That the Bill be now read the third time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"— The House divided; Ayes 38, Noes 77.

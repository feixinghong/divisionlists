Jovis, 9° die Junii, 1836.

Copyright (Ireland),—Motion made, and Question proposed,—" That Leave be given to bring in a Bill to extend the protection of Copyright in Prints and Engravings to Ireland :"—(Mr. Buckingham :)—Debate arising :—Motion made, and Question put,—" That the Debate be adjourned till this day week :"—(Sir Robert Harry Inglis :)—The House divided; Ayes 80, Noes 169.

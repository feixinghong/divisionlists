Lunæ , 20° die Junii, 1836.

Excise Licenses (Ireland) Bill,—read 3°; Clause added; Amendment proposed, in pr. 3, line 21, after Sunday, to insert "or at any time whatever on any Sunday, Good Friday, Christmas Day, or any day appointed for a public Fast or Thanksgiving:"—Question put, "That those words be there inserted:"—The House divided; Ayes 88, Noes 149.

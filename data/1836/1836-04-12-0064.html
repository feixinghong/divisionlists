Martis, 12° die Aprilis, 1836.

Landed Property,—Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That leave be given to bring in a Bill providing that in cases of intestacy, and in the absence of any settlement to the contrary, landed property be equally divided among the children or next of kin, of the deceased:"—(Mr. Ewart :)—Whereupon the Previous Question was proposed, "That that Question be now put:"—Previous Question, by leave, withdrawn:—Original Question put:—The House divided; Ayes 29, Noes 45.

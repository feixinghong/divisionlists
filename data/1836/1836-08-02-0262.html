Martis, 2° die Augusti, 1836.

Church of Ireland Bill,—Order for Consideration of Lords Amendments read ; Motion made, and Question proposed,—"That the Amendments be taken into Consideration upon this day three months:"—Amendment proposed, after the word " be " to insert the word " now," and to leave out the words "upon this day three months:"—Question proposed, "That the word 'now' be there inserted :"—Question put :—The House divided; Ayes 231, Noes 260.

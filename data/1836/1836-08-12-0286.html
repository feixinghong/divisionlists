Veneris, 12° die Augusti, 1836.

Jewish Civil Disabilities Bill,—Motion made, and Question proposed,— "That the Order of the Day for the Committee on the Bill be now read:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That" to the end of the Question, in order to add the words "this House will, upon this day six months, resolve itself into the said Committee," instead thereof:—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 42, Noes 3.

Duration of Speeches,-Motion made, and Question proposed, " That leave be given to bring in a Bill
for shortening the Duration ot Speeches in the Commons House of Parliament:"-(Major
Rasch :)-
And the Motion being opposed, after a brief explanatory statement from a Member who
opposed and from the Member who made the Motion, Mr. Speaker put the Question in pursuance,
of Standing Order 16:-The House divided; Ayes 167, Noes 100.

Light Railways Bill,-Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read a second time:"-
Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word "That," to the end of the Question,
in order to add the words " this House declines to sanction any scheme for the construction
of Litht Railways which will increase thé burden of local rates or add to the weight of
Imperial taxation,"-(Mr. Lough,)-instead thereof:-
Question proposed, " That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question:"-
After Debate thereon;
Mr. Balfour'rose in his place, and claimed to move, " That the Question be now put:"-
Question put, " That the Question be now put:"-The House divided; Ayes 215, Noes 67.

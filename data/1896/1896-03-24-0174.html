Tuesday, 24th March, 1896.

Numb. 65.

Chelsea Water (Transfer) Bill (by Order),—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Sir Frederick Dixon-Hartland:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now,' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 125, Noes 287.

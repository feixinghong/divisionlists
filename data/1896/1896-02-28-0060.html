B. M.

Friday, 28th February, 1896.

Numb. 22.

Supply,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee.)
Civil Services and Revenue Departments (Supplementary Estimates), 1895-6.
Class V.
15. Motion made, and Question proposed, "That a Supplementary sum, not exceeding £17,000, be granted to Her Majesty, to defray the Charge which will come in course of payment during the year ending on the 31st day of March 1896, for sundry Colonial Services, including certain Grants in Aid:"—
Whereupon Motion made, and Question put, "That a sum, not exceeding £2,000, be granted for the said Service:"— (Mr. Labouchere:)—The Committee divided; Ayes 72, Noes 250.

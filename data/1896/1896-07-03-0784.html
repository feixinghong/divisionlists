Friday, 3rd July, 1896.

Numb. 308.

East Surrey Water Bill [Lords] (by Order)—Order for Third Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read the third time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day three months:"—(Mr. Cubitt:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now,' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 175, Noes 77.

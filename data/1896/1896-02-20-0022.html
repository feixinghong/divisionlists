Thursday, 20th February, 1896.

Numb. 8.

Land Tax Commissioners' Names,—Motion made, and Question proposed, "That leave be given to bring in a Bill to appoint additional Commissioners for executing the Acts for granting a Land Tax and other Rates and Taxes:"—(Mr. Hanbury :)—
And the Motion being opposed, after a brief explanatory statement from the Member who made the Motion, Mr. Speaker put the Question in pursuance of Standing Order No. 16:—The House divided; Ayes 262, Noes 111.

Veneris, 14° die Augusti, 1846.

Numb. 100.

Supply,—considered in Committee:—
(In the Committee:)—
Motion made, and Question put,—"That a sum, not exceeding £20,000, be granted to Her Majesty, on account of the Works for enlarging and improving Buckingham Palace, in the year 1846:"—The Committee divided; Ayes 55, Noes 6.

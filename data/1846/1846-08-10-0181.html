Lunæ, 10° die Augusti, 1846.

Numb. 96.

Arms (Ireland) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read ; Motion made, and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day month :"—Question put, " That the word ' now' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 56, Noes 23.

Martis, 10° die Martii, 1846.

Numb. 9.

Frost, Williams, and Jones,—Motion made, and Question proposed, " That an humble Address be presented to Her Majesty, that She will be graciously pleased to take into Her Majesty's most gracious consideration of the Petitions of the People presented during the present Session of Parliament, in favour of a restoration to their native land of Frost, Williams, and Jones :"—(Mr. Thomas Duncombe :)— The House divided ; Ayes 31, Noes 196.

Numb. 98.

Religious Opinions Bill,—considered in Committee :—
(In the Committee :)—
First Clause;—(repealing certain parts of Acts 1 Eliz. c. 1, and 2 Eliz. c. 1 : Proviso, That nothing in this Enactment contained shall authorise or render it lawful for any person or persons to affirm, hold, stand with, set forth, maintain, or defend any such Foreign Power, pre-eminence, jurisdiction, or authority, nor shall the same extend further than to the repeal of the particular penalties and punishments herein referred to, but in all other respects the Law shall continue the same as if this Enactment had not been made) :—Amendment proposed in page 3, line 26, after the words " referred to," to insert the words " or consequent thereon :"—Question put, " That those words be there inserted :"—The Committee divided ; Ayes 10, Noes 53.

Veneris, 27° die Martii, 1846.

Numb. 27.

Corn Importation Bill,—Motion made, and Question proposed. "That the Bill be now read a second time :"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 302, Noes 214.

Veneris, 15° die Maii, 1846.

Numb. 47.

Corn Importation Bill,—Order read, for resuming adjourned Debate on Amendment proposed to be made to Question [11th May], "That the Bill be now read the third time," and which Amendment was to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question again proposed, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—Debate resumed:—Question put:—The House divided ; Ayes 327, Noes 229.

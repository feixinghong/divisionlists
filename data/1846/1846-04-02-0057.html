Numb. 30.

Jovis, 2° die Aprilis, 1846.

Factories,—Motion made, and Question put, "That a Select Committee be appointed, to inquire into the allegations of the Petition of the Chairman of a Meeting of Inhabitants of Dundee, complaining of illegal trial and imprisonment of Jane Bennet and other Factory Girls in October last:"— (Mr. Thomas Duncombe:) — The House divided; Ayes 38, Noes 63.

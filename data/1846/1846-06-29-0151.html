Lunæ, 29° die Junii, 1846.

Numb. 78.

Sheffield, Rotherham, Barnsley, Wakefield, Huddersfield, and Goole Railway Bill,—Motion made, Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read the third time:"—Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 146, Noes 53.

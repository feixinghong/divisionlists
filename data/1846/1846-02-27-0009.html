Veneris, 27° die Februarii, 1846.

Numb. 4.

Customs, and Corn Importation,—Order for Committee read ; Motion made, and Question proposed,— "That Mr. Speaker do now leave the Chair :"— Amendment proposed, to leave out from the word " That" to the end, of the Question, in order to add the words " this House will, upon this day six months, resolve itself into the said Committee," instead thereof :—Question put, "That the words proposed to be left out stand part of the Question :"—The House divided ; Ayes 337, Noes 240.

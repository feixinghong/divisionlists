Thursday, 6th May, 1886.

Numb. 88.

Crofters (Scotland) (No. 2) Bill,—As amended, considered:—
Another Amendment proposed, in page 13, line 35, after the word "on," to insert the words "or near:"—(Sir George Campbell:)—
Question put, "That the words 'or near' be there inserted:"—The House divided; Ayes 70, Noes 69.

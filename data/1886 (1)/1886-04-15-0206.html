Another Amendment proposed, in page 6, line 32, after the word "strading" to insert the following sub-section:—
"If the land form part of a deer, forest, and if the assignation of such land for the purposes of this Act would seriously impair the use of the reminmder as a deer forest, unless the Land Commission are satisfied that such result would on the whole increase the proserity of the inhabitants generally of the district in which such deer forest is situated:"—(Mr. Arthur Balfour:)—
Question proposed, "That those words be there inserted:—

Question, "That the words proposed to bo left out stand part of the proposed Amendment." put, and negatived.
Question, "That the words and would act injuriously on be there inserted." put and agreed to.

Thursday, 29th June, 1876.

Numb. 162.

Land Tenure (Ireland) Bil|,—Order read, for resuming Adjourned Debate on Amendment proposed to Question [29th march], "That the Bill be now read a second time;" and which Amendment was, to leave out the word: "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months;"—(Mr. Clive:)—
Question again proposed, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—Debate resume:—
Question put:—The House divided; Ayes, 56, Noes 290.

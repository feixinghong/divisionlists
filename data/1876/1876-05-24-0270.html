Wednesday, 24th May, 1876.

Numb. 113.

Wild Fowl Preservation Bill,—Order read, for resuming Adjourned Debate on Question [23rd February], "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Question again proposed:—Debate resumed:—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Mr. P. A. Taylor:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 337, Noes 13.

A. M.

Tuesday, 11th July, 1876.

Numb. 169.

Navy (Captain Sulivan),—Motion made, and Question put, " That, in the opinion of this House, Captain Sulivan should not have been removed from the command of one of Her Majesty's ships for any alleged error, shortcoming, or neglect of duty, without having been giving an opportunity, if he desired it, of explaining of defending his conduct before a competent court:"—
—(Mr. Ashley:)—The House divided ; Ayes 91, Noes 103.

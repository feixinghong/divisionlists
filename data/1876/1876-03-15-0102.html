B. M.

Wednesday, 15th March, 1876.

Numb. 50.

Church Rates Abolition (Scotland) Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill he now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months :"—(Sir William Cuninghame :)—
Question put, "That the word 'now' stand part of the Question:" —The House divided;Ayes 155, Noes 210.

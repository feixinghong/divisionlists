A. M.

Friday, 25th February, 1876.

Numb. 11.

Supply [18th February],—Postponed Resolutions [reported 23rd February] considered:—
Amendment proposed, to the Ninth Resolution, to leave out "£33,500," and insert " £31,500," —(Mr. James,)—instead thereof:—
Question put, "That '£33,500' stand part of the Resolution:"—The House divided; Ayes 137, Noes 71.

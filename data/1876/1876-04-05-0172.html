Wednesday, 5th April, 1876.

Numb. 77.

Elementary Education Act (1870) Amendment Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day six months:"—(Mr. Sandford:)—
Question put, " That the word ' now' stand part of the Question :"—The House divided; Ayes 160, Noes 281.

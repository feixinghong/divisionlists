Wednesday, 9th August, 1876.

Numb. 234.

Appellate Jurisdiction Bill [Lords],—As amended, considered :—
Another Amendment proposed, in page 5, line 21, to leave out from the word " Majesty " to th word "Whereas," in page 6, line 3 :—(Mr. Benesford Hope :)—
Question put, " That the words proposed to be left our stand part of the Bill :"—The House divided ; Ayes 55, Noes 42.

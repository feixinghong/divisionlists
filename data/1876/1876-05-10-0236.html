Wednesday, 10th May, 1876.

Numb. 102.

Barristers and Advocates Fees Bill,—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, " That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word " now," and at the end of the Question to add the words " upon this day six months:"—(Sir Henry Jackson:)—
Question put, " That the word ' now' stand part of the Question :"—The house divided; Ayes 130, Noes 237

Tuesday, 3rd March, 1896.

Numb. 26.

Metropolitan District Railway Bill (by Order),—Order for Second Reading read;
Motion made, and Question proposed, "That the Bill be now read a second time:"—
Amendment proposed, to leave out the word "now," and at the end of the Question to add the words "upon this day six months:"—(Mr. Richards:)—
Question put, "That the word 'now,' stand, part of the Question:"—The House divided; Ayes 154, Noes 95.
